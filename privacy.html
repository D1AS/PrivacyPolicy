📃 Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 25, 2025
App Name: Student Expense Tracker
Platform: iOS
Developer: Buckit

🔐 Your Privacy Matters

At Student Expense Tracker, your privacy is a top priority. This app is designed to help students manage their expenses securely, without unnecessary data collection or tracking.

📲 Information We Collect

The app itself does not collect any personal information from you directly.

However, we use Firebase, a backend service by Google, to provide cloud functionality such as:

  • 🔁 Cloud Sync of your expense data (optional)
  • 📊 Crash Reporting & Performance Monitoring
  • 🔒 Authentication – optional sign-in using Apple ID

Firebase May Collect:

  • Device type and OS version
  • Anonymous usage statistics
  • User ID (if sign-in is used)
  • IP address (temporarily)

We use Firebase with privacy-focused defaults:

  • No personalized ads
  • No third-party sharing
  • No access to contact, location, or photo data

🚫 What We Don't Do

  • ❌ No selling or sharing of your data
  • ❌ No in-app tracking or cross-site ads
  • ❌ No access to camera, microphone, location, or photos
  • ❌ No user profiling or behavioral analytics

🔄 Data Retention

If you sign in and sync your data, it is securely stored in your Firebase account linked to your Apple ID or email.

🇪🇺 GDPR & International Compliance

We only collect what's necessary. You have the right to request data access or deletion.

🔧 Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy for improvements or regulations. You'll be notified in-app of significant changes.
